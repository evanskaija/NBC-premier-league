FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How can I report incorrect scores?
You can use our contact form and select "General Support" or email support@ligikuu.co.tz with match details and correct evidence.
How can I advertise on the website?
For all commercial inquiries, please contact our advertising department at ads@ligikuu.co.tz to receive our media kit.
How can I submit football news?
Send your press releases and news tips to news@ligikuu.co.tz followed by your contact credentials.
How can I contact support?
Our support team is available via the contact form, live chat, or email. Standard response time is under 12 hours.
